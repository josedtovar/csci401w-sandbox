## Product Personas

### Persona 1

**\<Professor\>**

\<A professor who provides around-the-clock supplemental learning support for students using SmartLearnChat. Requires a consistent and customizable AI chatbot with responses based on custom material. The ability to asseses student understanding is also key>

### Persona 2

**\<student\>**

\<A student (user) that needs interactive, personalized tutoring. They are the primary users of the application. The students interact directly with the AI, and provide useful feed back for both the educators and administrators>

### Persona 3

**\<System Administrator\>**

\<The administrators role is to review and ensure the accuracy of the information in the tutorbot's output. They require a way to receive reported outputs, and a way to adjust the tutorbot.>
